About Us

Our History

Started in 2020, Ristorante con Fusion quickly established itself as a culinary icon par excellence in Srikakulam,Andhra Pradesh. With its unique brand of world fusion cuisine that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in Andhra Pradesh. Featuring four of the best three-star Michelin chefs in the world, you never know what will arrive on your plate the next time you visit us

The restaurant traces its humble beginnings to The Frying Pan, a successful chain started by our CEO, Miss. Kranthi Kumari Potnuru, that featured for the first time the world's best cuisines in a pan
